Top 5 road trip tips

By Michael. • 8 Mar 2025 • Travel
Home  •  Blog

Road trips are more fun when you arrive relaxed and prepared. Follow these five practical tips:

  1. Plan the route & rest stops. Map fuel stops, overnight stays and regular breaks — avoid long non-stop drives.
  2. Check the car thoroughly. Inspect tyre pressure & spare, oil, coolant, brakes, lights and battery. Address any warning lights before leaving.
  3. Pack smart essentials. Driver’s licence, vehicle documents, first-aid kit, basic toolkit, torch, phone charger/powerbank, water and snacks.
  4. Manage payments & communications. Carry some cash for remote areas, a working phone with roaming/data plan and share your itinerary with someone trusted.
  5. Safety & comfort first. Rotate drivers where possible, obey speed limits, secure valuables and confirm your insurance and roadside assistance cover.

Quick checklist: tyres, fluids, lights, phone charge, documents, and enough fuel to reach the next station.
